Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Click "Sign Up" on the login page, enter your email and password, and you'll receive an invite code. Enter the code to complete registration.

How do I start a chat?

Click the "+" button in the sidebar to create a new room or start a direct message with another user.

What is Wave Pro?

Wave Pro is our premium subscription that unlocks unlimited AI conversations, music uploads/downloads, and exclusive features.

Account & Settings

How do I change my password?

Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Change Password.

How do I upload a profile picture?

Go to your Profile page and click on your avatar to upload a new image.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Data & Storage → Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Can I change my username?

Yes, go to Settings → General → Username and update it.

Features

How do I use the AI assistant?

Click on the AI tab in the navigation. Type your message or use commands like /search, /weather, or /help.

How do I upload music?

Wave Pro users can go to the Music tab and click "Upload Track" to share MP3 files.

How do I add Telegram channels to my feed?

Go to the Feed tab, click "Add Channel", and paste the Telegram channel URL (t.me/channelname).

Can I edit or delete messages?

Yes, hover over your message and click the edit or delete icon.

How do I pin a message?

Hover over any message and click the pin icon. Pinned messages appear at the top of the chat.

Troubleshooting

Messages aren't sending

Try these steps:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Refresh the page
  • Clear browser cache
  • Try a different browser

Can't log in

Make sure you're using the correct email and password. If you forgot your password, use the "Forgot Password" link.

AI isn't responding

The AI service may be temporarily unavailable. Try again in a few minutes or check the AI status indicator.

Music won't play

Ensure your browser allows audio playback and check your device volume. Some browsers require user interaction before playing audio.
