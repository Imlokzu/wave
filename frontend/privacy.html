Privacy Policy

schedule Last updated: December 28, 2025

1 Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address
  • Username and display name
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Profile picture and bio

Usage Data

  • Messages and chat history
  • AI conversation history
  • Music uploads and playlists
  • Feed subscriptions
  • Login activity and session data

Technical Data

  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Connection timestamps

2 How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Process your messages and content
  • Enable AI chat features
  • Store and stream music
  • Manage your subscription and billing
  • Send service notifications
  • Improve and optimize the Service
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3 Data Storage and Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt
  • Data is stored on secure Supabase servers
  • Connections use HTTPS encryption
  • JWT tokens for secure authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates

4 Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Supabase (database), DeepSeek (AI services)
  • Other Users: Profile info, messages, and content you choose to share
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights

5 Third-Party Services

Wave Messenger integrates with:

  • DeepSeek AI: For AI chat features
  • Supabase: For data storage and authentication
  • Telegram: For feed aggregation (read-only)

These services have their own privacy policies that govern their use of your data.

6 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of notifications
  • Control profile visibility

7 Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • Personal data is deleted within 30 days
  • Messages in group chats may remain visible to other users
  • Backup copies are deleted within 90 days
  • Some data may be retained for legal compliance

8 Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and local storage to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Improve performance

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect functionality.

9 Children's Privacy

Wave Messenger is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we learn that we have collected data from a child, we will delete it promptly.

10 International Users

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. By using the Service, you consent to such transfers.

11 Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.

12 Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact us through the Help Center.
